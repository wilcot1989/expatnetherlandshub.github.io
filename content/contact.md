If you represent a company that serves expats in the Netherlands and want to discuss a partnership, sponsorship, or review opportunity, email me at the address above with the subject line "Partnership".

I only work with brands I would genuinely recommend. If your product does not meet that standard, I will politely decline.

## Found a Bug in a Tool?

If one of the [calculators or wizards](/tools/) is giving incorrect results, please let me know with:

- Which tool you were using
- What inputs you entered
- What result you got (and what you expected)

This helps me fix issues quickly. All tools are updated regularly to reflect the latest 2026 Dutch rules and thresholds.

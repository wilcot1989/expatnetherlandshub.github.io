      <h2>What Is Inburgering in the Netherlands?</h2>
      <p>
        Inburgering is the Dutch civic integration programme. It is a legal requirement for most non-EU immigrants who receive a permanent or long-term residence permit in the Netherlands. The word literally means \u201cbecoming a citizen\u201d (burger = citizen), and the programme is designed to help newcomers integrate into Dutch society by learning the language and understanding Dutch customs, laws, and institutions.
      </p>
      <p>
        Completing inburgering is not just a bureaucratic hurdle. Passing the exams can be required before you can apply for permanent residence or naturalisation. It is also directly tied to your DUO loan: pass within the deadline and the loan obligation may be reduced or forgiven.
      </p>

      <h2>Old System vs New System: 2013 vs 2021</h2>
      <p>
        The Netherlands overhauled its integration law in 2022. Understanding which law applies to you is the first step.
      </p>
      <table>
        <thead>
          <tr><th>Feature</th><th>Wet inburgering 2013</th><th>Wet inburgering 2021</th></tr>
        </thead>
        <tbody>
          <tr><td>Who it applies to</td><td>Arrivals before 1 Jan 2022</td><td>Arrivals from 1 Jan 2022</td></tr>
          <tr><td>Required Dutch level</td><td>A2</td><td>B1 (standard route)</td></tr>
          <tr><td>Routes available</td><td>One standard path</td><td>B1, Education, Z-Route</td></tr>
          <tr><td>Municipality role</td><td>Individual responsibility</td><td>Municipality guides you</td></tr>
          <tr><td>Deadline</td><td>3 years (extendable)</td><td>3 years (extendable)</td></tr>
          <tr><td>DUO loan</td><td>Up to &euro;10,000</td><td>Up to &euro;10,000</td></tr>
          <tr><td>Fine if not completed</td><td>Up to &euro;1,250</td><td>Up to &euro;1,250</td></tr>
        </tbody>
      </table>
      <div class="info-box">
        <p><strong>Important:</strong> If you arrived before 1 January 2022 but have not yet completed your inburgering, your municipality or DUO can tell you your exact deadline. Extensions are possible for illness, pregnancy, or circumstances beyond your control.</p>
      </div>

      <h2>The B1 Route Explained</h2>
      <p>
        The B1 Route (or inburgeringsroute) is the standard path under the new 2021 law. It is the route most newcomers follow. Here is what it involves:
      </p>
      <ul>
        <li><strong>Language exams (4 components):</strong> Reading (Lezen), Listening (Luisteren), Writing (Schrijven), and Speaking (Spreken) &mdash; all at B1 level on the Common European Framework of Reference (CEFR).</li>
        <li><strong>KNM &mdash; Kennis van de Nederlandse Maatschappij:</strong> Knowledge of Dutch Society exam, covering history, government, culture, and how Dutch institutions work.</li>
        <li><strong>MAP &mdash; Module Arbeidsmarkt &amp; Participatie:</strong> The Participation Declaration, covering Dutch values, anti-discrimination laws, and your rights and obligations as a resident.</li>
        <li><strong>ONA &mdash; Ori&euml;ntatie op de Nederlandse Arbeidsmarkt:</strong> Orientation on the Dutch labour market (applicable for most routes).</li>
      </ul>
      <p>
        Your municipality (gemeente) is responsible for guiding you through the process and connecting you with a language school that carries the <strong>Blik op Werk</strong> quality mark. You can also choose your own school from the Blik op Werk register.
      </p>

      <h2>How Much Does Inburgering Cost?</h2>
      <p>
        The cost of inburgering varies widely depending on your starting level, the language school you choose, and how many exam retakes you need. Here is a realistic breakdown:
      </p>
      <ul>
        <li><strong>Language course:</strong> &euro;3,000&ndash;&euro;7,000 depending on intensity, school, and starting level</li>
        <li><strong>Exam fees:</strong> approximately &euro;44 per exam component (6 components total = &euro;264 if you pass everything first time)</li>
        <li><strong>Study materials:</strong> &euro;100&ndash;&euro;300 for textbooks and practice software</li>
        <li><strong>Total realistic cost:</strong> &euro;4,000&ndash;&euro;8,000 from A0 level</li>
      </ul>
      <div class="info-box">
        <p><strong>DUO loan:</strong> DUO (Dienst Uitvoering Onderwijs) offers a loan of up to &euro;10,000 to cover inburgering costs. The loan is repaid over 10 years at a low interest rate. If you successfully complete the programme within the deadline, a portion of the loan can be forgiven depending on your income. Apply via <a href="https://www.duo.nl" target="_blank" rel="noopener">duo.nl</a>.</p>
      </div>

      <h2>Tips for Passing the Inburgering Exam</h2>
      <p>
        Having helped many expats through this process, here is what consistently makes the difference:
      </p>
      <ul>
        <li><strong>Start early.</strong> Three years sounds like a lot of time, but language learning takes consistent effort. Starting immediately gives you a buffer for life events and retakes.</li>
        <li><strong>Choose a Blik op Werk school.</strong> Schools with the Blik op Werk quality mark are officially approved and held to high standards. Avoid unregistered schools.</li>
        <li><strong>Use daily practice apps.</strong> Apps like Babbel or Duolingo are not sufficient on their own, but 15&ndash;20 minutes of daily practice dramatically accelerates your classroom learning.</li>
        <li><strong>Practise the KNM seriously.</strong> Many people focus only on language and underestimate the KNM exam. It covers specific Dutch history and institutions &mdash; use the official study materials from <a href="https://www.inburgeren.nl" target="_blank" rel="noopener">inburgeren.nl</a>.</li>
        <li><strong>Book exams in advance.</strong> Exam slots fill up quickly in busy municipalities. Book your slot as soon as your school says you are ready &mdash; do not wait until the last minute.</li>
        <li><strong>Request an extension early if needed.</strong> If you have a legitimate reason (illness, pregnancy, work disruption), apply for an extension through your municipality before your deadline passes &mdash; not after.</li>
      </ul>

  // ─────────────────────────────────────────────
  //  RADIO INTERACTION
  // ─────────────────────────────────────────────
  function initRadios() {
    document.querySelectorAll('.radio-option input[type="radio"]').forEach(function(input) {
      input.addEventListener('change', function() {
        var group = this.closest('.radio-group');
        group.querySelectorAll('.radio-option').forEach(function(opt) {
          opt.classList.remove('selected');
        });
        this.closest('.radio-option').classList.add('selected');
        state[this.name] = this.value;
      });
    });
  }

  // ─────────────────────────────────────────────
  //  EXEMPTION CHECK
  // ─────────────────────────────────────────────
  function checkExemption() {
    var reasons = [];
    if (state.nationality === 'eu') {
      reasons.push('EU / EEA / Swiss citizen');
    }
    if (state.nationality === 'exempted') {
      reasons.push('Nationality from exempted country');
    }
    if (state.age === 'under18') {
      reasons.push('Under 18 years old');
    }
    if (state.age === '65plus') {
      reasons.push('65 years or older');
    }
    if (state.education === 'dutch') {
      reasons.push('Holds a Dutch diploma (MBO level 2+)');
    }
    return reasons;
  }

  // ─────────────────────────────────────────────
  //  DETERMINE ROUTE
  // ─────────────────────────────────────────────
  function determineRoute() {
    if (state.arrival === 'before2022') {
      return 'old2013';
    }
    // New system (after Jan 2022 or not yet arrived)
    if (state.education === 'none' && state.goal !== 'study') {
      return 'zroute';
    }
    if (state.goal === 'study') {
      return 'education';
    }
    return 'b1';
  }

  // ─────────────────────────────────────────────
  //  NAVIGATION
  // ─────────────────────────────────────────────
  function goToStep2() {
    if (!state.arrival || !state.nationality || !state.age || !state.education) {
      alert('Please answer all four questions before continuing.');
      return;
    }

    // Check exemption first
    var exemptReasons = checkExemption();
    if (exemptReasons.length > 0) {
      document.getElementById('step1').classList.add('hidden');
      showExempt(exemptReasons);
      setDot(3);
      scrollToIndicator();
      gtag('event', 'wizard_complete', { route_type: 'exempt', reasons: exemptReasons.join(',') });
      return;
    }

    document.getElementById('step1').classList.add('hidden');
    document.getElementById('step2').classList.remove('hidden');
    setDot(2);
    scrollToIndicator();
    gtag('event', 'wizard_step', { step: 2, arrival: state.arrival, nationality: state.nationality });
  }

  function goToStep1() {
    document.getElementById('step2').classList.add('hidden');
    document.getElementById('step1').classList.remove('hidden');
    setDot(1);
    scrollToIndicator();
  }

  function showExempt(reasons) {
    var el = document.getElementById('exemptSection');
    el.classList.remove('hidden');

    var tagsEl = document.getElementById('exemptTags');
    tagsEl.innerHTML = reasons.map(function(r) {
      return '<span class="exempt-tag">' + r + '</span>';
    }).join('');

    if (state.nationality === 'eu') {
      document.getElementById('exemptReason').textContent = 'As an EU, EEA, or Swiss citizen, you have the right to live and work in the Netherlands without completing inburgering.';
    } else if (state.nationality === 'exempted') {
      document.getElementById('exemptReason').textContent = 'Your nationality is on the list of countries exempt from the Dutch inburgering requirement. You do not need to follow the programme.';
    } else if (state.age === 'under18') {
      document.getElementById('exemptReason').textContent = 'You are under 18, so you are not required to complete inburgering. This changes if you are still in the Netherlands after your 18th birthday.';
    } else if (state.age === '65plus') {
      document.getElementById('exemptReason').textContent = 'People aged 65 and older are exempt from the inburgering requirement in the Netherlands.';
    } else if (state.education === 'dutch') {
      document.getElementById('exemptReason').textContent = 'Holding a Dutch diploma at MBO level 2 or higher exempts you from inburgering. You have already demonstrated integration through the Dutch education system.';
    }
  }

  function showResults() {
    if (!state.dutch || !state.goal) {
      alert('Please answer both questions before continuing.');
      return;
    }

    document.getElementById('step2').classList.add('hidden');
    var resultsSection = document.getElementById('resultsSection');
    resultsSection.classList.add('visible');
    setDot(3);
    scrollToIndicator();

    var route = determineRoute();
    buildResults(route);

    gtag('event', 'wizard_complete', {
      route_type: route,
      dutch_level: state.dutch,
      goal: state.goal,
      arrival: state.arrival
    });
  }

  function resetWizard() {
    state.arrival = null;
    state.nationality = null;
    state.age = null;
    state.education = null;
    state.dutch = null;
    state.goal = null;

    document.querySelectorAll('input[type="radio"]').forEach(function(i) { i.checked = false; });
    document.querySelectorAll('.radio-option').forEach(function(el) { el.classList.remove('selected'); });

    document.getElementById('resultsSection').classList.remove('visible');
    document.getElementById('exemptSection').classList.add('hidden');
    document.getElementById('step2').classList.add('hidden');
    document.getElementById('step1').classList.remove('hidden');
    setDot(1);
    scrollToIndicator();
  }

  // ─────────────────────────────────────────────
  //  STEP DOTS
  // ─────────────────────────────────────────────
  function setDot(active) {
    for (var i = 1; i <= 3; i++) {
      var dot = document.getElementById('dot' + i);
      dot.classList.remove('active', 'done');
      if (i < active) dot.classList.add('done');
      else if (i === active) dot.classList.add('active');
    }
    if (active > 1) document.getElementById('line1').classList.add('done');
    else document.getElementById('line1').classList.remove('done');
    if (active > 2) document.getElementById('line2').classList.add('done');
    else document.getElementById('line2').classList.remove('done');
  }

  function scrollToIndicator() {
    window.scrollTo({ top: document.getElementById('stepIndicator').offsetTop - 80, behavior: 'smooth' });
  }

  function buildResults(routeKey) {
    var data = routeData[routeKey];
    var duration = data.durations[state.dutch] || data.durations['a0'];

    });
    schoolsHtml += '</ul>';

    var linksHtml = '<ul class="links-list">';
    data.links.forEach(function(link) {
      linksHtml +=
        '<li><a href="' + link.url + '" target="_blank" rel="noopener">' + link.label + '</a></li>';

      '<li><a href="/guides/integration/inburgeringsexamen-preparation-guide-2026/">Full inburgering guide on ExpatNetherlandsHub</a></li>';

      // Row 1

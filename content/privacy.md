# Privacy Policy

*Last updated: February 2026*

## Who We Are

Expat Netherlands Hub is an independent resource website for expats moving to and living in the Netherlands.

## What Data We Collect

### Analytics
We use Google Analytics 4 to anonymously measure website usage. This helps us improve our content. No personally identifiable information is stored.

### Cookies
We use functional cookies and analytics cookies. You can manage your preferences via our cookie banner.

### Affiliate Links
When you click an affiliate link, the respective provider may place cookies to track the referral. Please consult the privacy policy of the respective provider.

## Your Rights (GDPR)
You have the right to access, correct, and delete your personal data. Contact us via our contact page.

## Contact
For privacy-related questions, please reach out via our contact page.

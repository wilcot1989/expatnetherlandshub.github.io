## Privacy Policy

*Last updated: February 2026*

### 1. Who We Are

Expat Netherlands Hub (expatnetherlandshub.com) is an independent guide for expats living in and moving to the Netherlands.

### 2. What Data We Collect

We do not directly collect personal data. When you visit our website, the following data may be automatically collected via analytics cookies (only with your consent):

- IP address (anonymized)
- Browser type and version
- Pages visited and time spent
- Referring website

### 3. Cookies

We use cookies for:

- **Analytics cookies** (Google Analytics) — Only with your explicit consent. These cookies help us understand how visitors use our website.
- **Functional cookies** — To store your cookie preferences.

You can withdraw your consent at any time by clearing your browser data.

### 4. Affiliate Links

Our website contains affiliate links to external services. When you click these links, the respective party may place cookies. Please refer to the privacy policy of the respective service for more information.

### 5. Third Parties

We do not share personal data with third parties, except for analytics data collected by Google Analytics (with your consent).

### 6. Your Rights

Under the GDPR, you have the right to:

- Request access to your data
- Request correction or deletion
- Object to processing
- Withdraw your consent

### 7. Contact

For privacy questions, please contact us at [info@expatnetherlandshub.com](mailto:info@expatnetherlandshub.com).
